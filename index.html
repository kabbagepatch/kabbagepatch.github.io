Books


Many times in college I have tried to read more. Every time it would go something like this: I'd decide on reading, go to the library and check out a book, read it in a couple weeks, return it and never check another again. When 2016 started, I had vaguely heard of the 52 book challenge and decided to do it. I failed miserably. I read five books.

But, once I graduated and started working, I had more free time. The lack of homework and something intellectual to do in my evenings, I thought I should take advantage of this and attempt the same challenge again. Things turned out differently. I started reading regularly, and one book at a time, I succeeded. It was almost unbelievable. The feeling was exhilarating. Going from 5 to 55 was incredible.
